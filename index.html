Who we are..

We are four young people, who want to make an impact on the environment to tackle the problem of food waste. That is why we created Bread n' Bites, a delicious treat that is made out of otherwise wasted bread. As food lovers we wanted to create something delicious while also working towards a more sustainable economy.

What we do..

Our mission is to reduce food waste.

Did you know that each year 25% of the produced bread are wasted? This equal around 70 million kg of bread.

We want to tackle this problem.
Our solution is making a delicious treat out of wasted bread.
We collect these breads from different bakeries and make our delicious prouct Bread ‘n Bites of it.
